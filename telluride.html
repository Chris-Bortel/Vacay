Telluride

Have an itch for adventure? Then look no further than Telluride, Colorado!



From world class skiing in the winters to world-famous festivals in the summer, this idyllic mountain town has something for everyone!

Founded as a small mining outpost in the late 1800’s, this picturesque box canyon became a travel destination for celebrities and ski-bums alike when the ski came into existence in the late 70’s.

Being at least a 6 hour drive from Denver, Telluride’s remote location make it a destination for the determined. While Vail and other front-range resorts take much of the incoming ski-traffic, only those willing to make the trek make it to Telluride. This keeps lift lines to a reasonable size and allows the town to retain much of its original rustic charm.

And if skiing isn’t for you, there’s plenty else to do! The Telluride Film Festival draws the Hollywood elite in the summer, while the Bluegrass music festival keeps the vibe rooted. World class Ice Climbing is just a two-hour drive away in Ouray Colorado, while the majestic Moab Desert and Indian Creek provide regular climber’s a paradise of rock and mountain biking.
