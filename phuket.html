Need some fun in the sun? Then take a trip to the stars and visit Los Angeles, California!



But don’t just go straight to Hollywood. The sprawl of this city can be intimidating. Visitors are often overwhelmed and stuck seeing nothing but the fake movie sets barely covering the growing homelessness crisis.

Dive deeper to visit the artistic epicenter that made Los Angeles what it is today. From the beaches on the Westside near Santa Monica to the hippest coffee shops in town on the east-side, discover the magic that lies within each unique neighborhood.

Silver Lake, Echo Park, and Los Feliz make up a cultural tri-fecta on the eastside of the city. With dozens of boutique music venues drawing major acts into intimate settings, or discovering the next big thing, there’s always something to do in this favorite haunt for the creative spirit in LA.

Visit Pasadena in the foothills of the San Gabriel mountains to catch a game at the famous Rose-Bowl stadium. Better yet, head over to Lucky Boy for their world famous breakfast burrito!

Take a hike through the Griffith Observatory and catch sunset from one of the best views at the top. Your friends will never believe there was so much greenery to be found in Los Angeles.

And yes, if you really need to, you can visit Hollywood too!
