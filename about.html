I Am Ashley--I'm First Because of my "A"--I'm Learning Python and Other Stuff


My Name is Ashley and I’m a Software Developer! My love for technology started when I worked for Apple as a Genius back in 2008. After 5 years of working at Apple, I Joined the Navy where I spent ~7 years doing construction (2.5 years) and working on submarines, in particular the USS Michigan (SSGN 727), as a Fire Control Specialist (FT). While working as an FT I was a system administrator for our classified networks on board as well as an operator for our weapons systems. I separated from the Navy in November 2019 which led me to my current position as a Site Reliability Engineer for Oracle Cloud Infrastructure. What I love most about software development is the challenge it gives me to create solutions for everyday issues! All my adult life I have looked for a job where there was always something to learn and software development has proven to be it.
